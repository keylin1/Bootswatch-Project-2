Victorian Escape Room


INT. VICTORIAN HOUSE - DAY

A tense atmosphere fills the Victorian house where our protagonist, with long brown curly hair and a darker skin tone, finds herself held captive along with other girls.

PROTAGONIST (V.O.): Spontaneous, yet intriguing, our ordeal begins.

They discover the Duke's obsession with people sharing their features through a vintage chart.

PROTAGONIST (V.O.) (CONT'D): The Duke's fixation became our nightmare.

With a looming sense of danger, they devise an escape plan.


EXT. VICTORIAN HOUSE - DAY

Seeking refuge, they hide in a half-basement closet, discovering a hidden door.

PROTAGONIST (V.O.) (CONT'D): We knew we had to escape, no matter the risks.

The protagonist skillfully unlocks the door with complex mechanisms, granting them freedom.


EXT. VICTORIAN TOWN - DAY

They dash through the old Victorian cobblestone streets, pursued by the Duke.

PROTAGONIST (V.O.) (CONT'D): The chase through the streets felt like a race against time.

Taking shelter in a sportswear store, they disguise themselves, evading capture.

PROTAGONIST (V.O.) (CONT'D): Disguised, we blended into the crowd, but danger still lurked.

A tense moment unfolds as a horse-drawn carriage approaches, signaling their continued peril.


FADE OUT.
